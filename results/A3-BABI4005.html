# Plot a count plot of Preferred Communication by Age
plt.figure(figsize=(12, 6))
sns.countplot(data=df, x='Age', hue='Preferred_Communication', palette='viridis')
plt.title('Preferred Communication by Age')
plt.xlabel('Preferred Communication')
plt.ylabel('Count')
plt.xticks(rotation=45)
plt.legend(title='Age', bbox_to_anchor=(1.05, 1), loc='upper left')
plt.tight_layout()
  • Text appears to be the most popular form of communication across all age groups, with consistently high counts. This suggests that users, regardless of age, prefer written communication for its convenience and ease.
  • Voice notes show a moderate preference, particularly among younger users (ages 18-24). This may indicate a trend towards more informal and expressive communication styles among younger demographics.
  • Video calls seem to be less preferred compared to texting and voice notes, but there may be a gradual increase in preference as the age increases, suggesting that older users might lean towards video communication more than younger users.
